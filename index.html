Petropavlovsk

In the Republic of Kazakhstan, the city of Petropavlovsk serves as the center of the North Kazakhstan region. It is located on the right bank of the longest tributary of the Irtysh - the Ishim River.

Like other cities in Kazakhstan, Petropavlovsk is an industrial city specializing in:

  1. heavy engineering
  2. production of railway gondola cars
  3. metal structures
  4. building materials and other production

Nationalities living in Petropavlovsk:

  • Russians (58.09%)
  • Kazakhs (31.13%)
  • Tatars (2.93%)
  • Ukrainians (1.99%)
  • Germans (1.98%)
